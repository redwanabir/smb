ABOUT

ONLINE STORE FOR LOCAL SMALL AND MEDIUM BUSINESS

Learn more about us

Millions of small merchants in Bangladesh are moving their businesses online, but great e-commerce tools aimed at these small businesses do not exist. We aim to combine world-class engineering with local know-how to create an e-commerce platform well-adapted to the unique needs of these merchants.

Many peoples do business through Facebook, WhatsApp. Our goal is to give them a platform managing their business through s site. Where their entire product is listed.

A link will be provided to share their store link with the customers. Where they can track of their business, they will be able to maintain their channel on their own.
